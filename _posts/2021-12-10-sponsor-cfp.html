Levels of Sponsorship

Platinum (USD 15000-25000)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Physical and virtual booth space (in case of hybrid conference)
  • 4 complimentary registration
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop, or demonstration
  • Choose two of the named events/awards
  • Engagement with researchers from the community during the conference (for online/hybrid conference)

Gold (USD 10000-14000)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Physical and virtual booth space (in case of hybrid conference)
  • 2 complimentary registration
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop, or demonstration
  • Choose one of the named events/awards
  • Engagement with researchers from the community during the conference (for online/hybrid conference)

Silver (USD 5000-9000)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • Access to opt-in database of attendee profile information who are interested in being recruited
  • Physical and Virtual booth space (in case of hybrid conference)
  • 1 complimentary registration
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop or demonstration
  • Engagement with researchers from the community during the conference (for online/hybrid conference)

Bronze (USD 2500-4000)

  • Company name and logo displayed on website and before plenary talks
  • 1 complimentary registration
  • EXPO – opportunity to apply to have a talk, workshop or demonstration
  • Engagement with researchers from the community during the conference (for online/hybrid conference)

Sponsorship Opportunities for Named Events and Awards

  • Best Paper Award
  • Best Student Paper Award
  • Invited Speaker
  • Best Demo Award
