Gather Town Instructions

The preferred way of attending the conference is our virtual conference center on the Gather platform. All main-conference sessions, as well as workshops and tutorials, will be done in Zoom.

Everything is accessible from inside gather.town. In particular, you can walk into sessions by entering one of the main track rooms, "Track A" and "Track B", just like you usually would. You can thus choose to spend the entire conference in Gather if you wish. The web page accompanies this with overview information as well as details on specific events.
