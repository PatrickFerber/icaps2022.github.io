Zoom Meetings

All technical and plenary sessions, as well as workshops, tutorials, and the DC will be held in Zoom.

In case you do not want to enter these sessions via gather.town (which is our recommended way of attending the conference), you can directly access the Zoom meetings listed below.

Guidelines for Zoom during the main conference

The main conference sessions will be run by Slideslive, our external partner, and will be moderated by a session chair. Slideslive will stream the pre-recorded videos into the Zoom meeting so that everybody watches the talks together. The preferred way of asking questions is by doing so directly via audio/video in Zoom, please use the "raise hand" feature of Zoom to ease the life of the session chair.

Note that all technical sessions and invited talks are recorded and will be made available online after the conference.

Password
